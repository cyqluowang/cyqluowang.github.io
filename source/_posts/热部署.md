title: 热部署
date: 2017-02-22 17:44:59
tags: spring
---
1、引入依赖
```
<dependency>
            <groupId>org.springframework</groupId>
            <artifactId>springloaded</artifactId>
</dependency>
```
2、修改插件
```
<build>
        <plugins>
            <plugin>
                <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
                <artifactId>spring-boot-maven-plugin</artifactId>
                <dependencies>
                    <!-- spring热部署-->
                    <dependency>
                        <groupId>org.springframework</groupId>
                        <artifactId>springloaded</artifactId>
                        <version>1.2.6.RELEASE</version>
                    </dependency>
                </dependencies>
            </plugin>
        </plugins>
</build>
```

3、修改编译器
** 打开自动编译

4、启动项目
```
mvn spring-boot:run
```
